# Privacy Policy for MicroFocus Zones

**Last Updated:** December 2, 2025

## Overview

MicroFocus Zones is a Chrome extension designed to help you manage tabs and focus time using task-scoped capsules, semantic gates, and micro-focus timers. This privacy policy explains how the extension handles your data.

## Data Collection and Storage

### Local Storage Only
MicroFocus Zones stores all data **locally on your device** using Chrome's storage API. No data is transmitted to external servers or third parties.

### Types of Data Stored Locally
- **Tab Information:** URLs, titles, and grouping information for tabs within your focus zones
- **Timer Settings:** Your configured focus timer durations and alarm preferences
- **Zone Configurations:** Your custom zone settings and preferences
- **Tab Group Data:** Information about tab groups you create and manage

## Permissions Explained

### Required Permissions
- **tabs:** Required to manage and organize your browser tabs into focus zones
- **storage:** Required to save your zones, settings, and preferences locally
- **sidePanel:** Required to display the extension's interface in the browser sidebar
- **scripting:** Required to inject content scripts for visual indicators and zone management
- **alarms:** Required for micro-focus timer functionality and notifications
- **tabGroups:** Required to create and manage tab groups for organizing zones
- **notifications:** Required to alert you when timers complete or zone transitions occur
- **host_permissions (<all_urls>):** Required to apply zone functionality across all websites you visit

## Data Usage

All data processed by MicroFocus Zones:
- Remains on your local device
- Is never transmitted over the internet
- Is never shared with the developer or any third parties
- Is never used for analytics, tracking, or advertising

## Data Security

Your data is protected by:
- Chrome's built-in storage encryption
- No network transmission (completely offline functionality)
- No external API calls or server connections

## Data Deletion

You can delete all extension data at any time by:
1. Removing the extension from Chrome
2. Clearing the extension's storage through Chrome settings

All data is permanently deleted when the extension is uninstalled.

## Third-Party Services

MicroFocus Zones does **not** use any third-party services, analytics, or tracking tools.

## Children's Privacy

This extension does not knowingly collect any information from users. It is safe for users of all ages.

Any changes to this privacy policy will be reflected in this document with an updated "Last Updated" date.
